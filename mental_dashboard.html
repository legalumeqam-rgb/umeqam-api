Safe Messaging Quick Reference
AFSP / SAMHSA / WHO Guidelines
✓ Always Include
  • Crisis line numbers (988, Samaritans)
  • Encourage professional consultation
  • "You are not alone" framing
  • Person-first language
  • Appropriate clinical terminology
  • Referral to qualified professionals
✗ Never Include
  • Method disclosure for self-harm
  • Romanticization of suicide
  • Stigmatizing language (crazy, psycho)
  • "Committed suicide" → use "died by suicide"
  • Definitive AI diagnosis
  • Advice against medical consultation
⚠ Review Required
  • Content involving minors (UN CRC)
  • Specific dosage or medication advice
  • Mental health data collection (GDPR Art.9)
  • Outdated diagnostic terminology
  • Pseudoscientific treatment claims
  • High-certainty diagnostic statements
